My Story

Here are some of my favorite memories and special moments from my life so far!

Living with Grandparents in Stockton, MO

I always played with chalk on the driveway, which was so much fun! I would watch movies with Mae and Ryan a lot. We did planks and push-ups with Dad to stay fit during COVID. I loved trying to use a hula-hoop or a jump-rope, even though I wasn't very good at it!

Pumpkin Patch Adventure

We went to a pumpkin patch in the fall and there was a pumpkin catapult that they allowed us to trigger. I was leaning forward in the wrong place when I pulled the trigger and the catapult whacked me right in the face! I was okay but it was scary.

We also got to pet kittens, goats, and puppies too. We got to pick out our own pumpkin and we did other fun games like pumpkin bowling and a corn maze.

Japanese Cousin Visits America

My Japanese cousin, Izumi, visited us from Japan by surprise! She brought us special snacks from Japan that were super tasty. We had to communicate using Google Translate, which was very fun because we would joke and laugh and tease Daeron too.

She would use silly face filters on me, so I did it back to her for fun!

Drinking Shirley Temples

One of my favorite treats - enjoying delicious Shirley Temples with friends and family!

Christmas with Grandparents

I loved going to visit my grandparents for Christmas because grandma always knew how to make a great Christmas meal. I loved petting her two dogs too: Thor (a German Shepherd) and Freya (a huge Malamute).

The decorations at grandma's house are always so pretty!

My 11th Birthday Party (with Dad)

I got to invite my very special friends and I was so happy that they came! We ran around a lot. The cake was good, the food was good, and I climbed a tree.

It was special to me because I was able to help plan this one instead of it being planned for me.

My 9th Birthday Party (with Mom)

I learned how to roller skate at Rink Rats! One of my friends there taught me how to sketch and draw.

Meeting My Softball Friends

Dad signed me up to play softball and I was so happy! We would play around so much. The first two weeks, I couldn't remember their names, but then I got used to them and remembered. We would have so much fun, but we did have our serious moments while practicing too.

Joining Franklin Smith Elementary

What I liked about starting school here was how everybody would make me feel welcome. We would be assigned "building jobs" where we had the chance to be responsible and help out around the school building.

The teachers there were very very nice to me and I made lots of friends there!
